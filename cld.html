IT Cohort Strategy 2026

Montreal IT Services Briefing

The Short Version

IT enrollment didn't drop because demand vanished; it dropped because our primary recruitment levers were removed. Between the -64% crash in international permits and the AI anxiety felt by entry-level domestic students, the old model is broken. This dashboard explores the data behind these shifts and outlines a transition toward short, high-impact professional modules funded by Services Québec and the Defence Industrial Strategy.

The 2026 Pivot Plan

"Fewer long runways, more short flights."

Short Professional Modules

Shift the center of gravity to 15-45 hour blocks. The AEC is the destination, but the short course is the front door. This targets the growing demographic: workers 30+, career-changers, and recent immigrants with PR needing local credentials.

Why we can't wait

Institutional quotas are fixed until December 2027. The PEQ pathway is gone. No new tuition exemptions. Waiting for the caps to lift is a 2029 conversation. The requalification market is standing outside our door right now.
